Frequently Asked Questions

Questions? We've Got Answers!

You probably have questions about our services. Here are some answers that may be useful to you.

What is your guarantee that your customers will be satisfied with your work?

Our staff are trained to do their work to give you 100% SATISFACTION GUARANTEE. If you’re unhappy with our cleaning and feel as though our home cleaning professionals missed anything – let us know within 24 hours and we’ll come back and clean it for FREE.

How long do regular cleanings take?

That depends on the house. For your example, a two-bedroom, two-bathroom condominium takes up to 3 hours to clean.

Do you charge by the hour?

Yes. $25 per hour.

How much does it cost to clean my home?

It is $25 per hour. And we do flat rate too! Please send us an inquiry in our contact page so that we can discuss this with you.

Are pets at home OK?

That's not a problem! We love pets!

How does payment work?

We accept all kinds of payment methods - cash, debit, credit, and paypal.

Do you do linens or laundry?

Linens and laundry is not part of our standard cleaning and as such, is not included in the timing estimate. However, if you would like linens and laundry done during our visit, please request it in the job notes and ensure you increase the timing of our visit to accommodate for the extra task.

Do I need to be home for the cleaning?

It is not necessary. It totally up to you! We ensure that our staff are all trustworthy!

How do I schedule a cleaning?

Simply by sending an email or text or by calling us.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule an appointment?

Just send us an email, text or call to let us know.

What if I don’t have a vacuum? Or a mop or a broom?

For your convenience, we supply absolutely everything. Our home cleaning professionals use only the highest quality products and the best equipment possible to ensure everything is done properly. The only event in which we may not provide products is if a customer has special requests and wants us to use something they provide.

What if something is broken or damaged during cleaning?

At Sparkly Bubbles Cleaning, we treat every single home we clean with the utmost care and respect. In the event something happens to break during our routine services, we’ll do our best to repair or replace the item.

Can I be part of the cleaning crew?

Yes you can! For questions regarding employment, please go to our contact page and select "Employment" as your subject. Kindly give your name and email and other contact info and we will try to contact you as soon as possible!
